## 4. FAQ Page Content *(جديد بالكامل)*

**Q: What exactly is a dahabiya?**
A: A dahabiya is a traditional Egyptian sailing vessel that has traveled the Nile for centuries. The word means "golden" in Arabic — a reference to the gilded boats once used by Egyptian royalty. VEDA's dahabiyas are solar-powered modern interpretations of this ancient form, built for comfort without sacrificing character.

**Q: Is the entire boat private?**
A: Yes. Every VEDA charter is fully private. You book the entire boat for your group — no other guests, no shared spaces, no strangers at breakfast. The crew, the schedule, and every meal are exclusively yours.

**Q: What is included in the charter rate?**
A: Everything onboard: all cabins, all meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner), soft drinks, fresh juices, filtered water, the full crew (captain, chef, hospitality team), and all onboard spaces including yoga deck, lounges, and sundecks. See the full list on our homepage.

**Q: What is NOT included?**
A: International flights, Egypt visa and travel insurance, temple and site entry fees, alcoholic beverages, spa treatments or massage, and crew gratuities. We can help arrange all of the above.

**Q: How many people can each boat accommodate?**
A: Veda One and Two: up to 20 guests. Veda Three: up to 10 guests. Veda Four: up to 28 guests. We can arrange multi-boat charters for larger groups.

**Q: What is the best time of year to sail?**
A: October through April is ideal — warm days, cool evenings, and clear skies. July and August are very hot and we don't recommend sailing then. November to February is our most popular season.

**Q: Can you accommodate dietary requirements?**
A: Always. Our chefs are experienced with vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and most other requirements. Just let us know when you enquire.

**Q: How do I book?**
A: Send us an enquiry through the Reserve page with your dates, group size, and preferred boat. We'll confirm availability and send a full proposal within 24 hours. A deposit secures your dates; final payment is due before departure.

**Q: What is the cancellation policy?**
A: We understand that travel plans change. Full details are included in your booking agreement. Generally: cancellations 60+ days before departure receive a full refund; 30–60 days receive 50%; under 30 days are non-refundable. We always try to find a solution.

**Q: Are children welcome?**
A: Absolutely. Families are among our favorite groups. There are no age restrictions onboard. We ask that parents supervise young children around the open decks, as the Nile is a working river.

**Q: Is the Nile safe to swim in?**
A: We don't recommend swimming in the Nile due to currents and river traffic. However, we can arrange stops at safe, calm riverside beaches on some routes.

**Q: Do you offer shore excursions?**
A: Temple visits are part of every itinerary and your crew will accompany you. For deeper Egyptology tours, licensed guides can be arranged at an additional cost. We can also organize hot air balloon flights over Luxor, felucca rides, and village visits.
