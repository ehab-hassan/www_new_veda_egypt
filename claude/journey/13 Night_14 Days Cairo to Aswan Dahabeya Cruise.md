Cairo to Aswan Dahabeya Cruise

13 Nights / 14 Days

Sail the legendary Nile from Cairo to Aswan aboard a traditional Dahabeya, elegantly towed by a support tugboat for an authentic and leisurely cruising experience. Throughout your journey, enjoy daily international buffet breakfast, lunch, and dinner, in addition to a daily sunset afternoon tea with snacks served on deck while sailing or during scenic moorings along the Nile banks.

Please note: As this itinerary sails southbound against the Nile current, estimated sailing times are approximately 15–20% longer than standard downstream itineraries.



Day 1 – Arrival in Cairo

Arrival at Cairo International Airport followed by transfer to your hotel or directly to your Dahabeya for embarkation.
Welcome dinner onboard.
Overnight: Cairo

Day 2 – Cairo Sightseeing

Explore the legendary Giza Pyramids and the treasures of the Egyptian Museum.
Enjoy all meals onboard including sunset afternoon tea with snacks.
Overnight: Cairo

Day 3 – Sail to Beni Suef

Begin your Nile journey sailing south toward Beni Suef through the fertile landscapes of Middle Egypt.

Distance: Approx. 120 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 17–18 hours(against the current)
Relax onboard while enjoying panoramic Nile scenery.
Overnight: Beni Suef

Day 4 – Fayoum Excursion

Excursion to the ancient Hawara Pyramid and Meidum Pyramid.
Sunset tea and dinner served onboard.
Overnight: Beni Suef

Day 5 – Sail to Minya / Tuna El Gebel & Ashmunein

Continue sailing south toward Minya followed by visits to Tuna El Gebel and Ashmunein.

Distance: Approx. 145 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 20–21 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Minya

Day 6 – Tell El Amarna & Beni Hassan

Explore Tell el-Amarna including the Northern Tombs and the Tomb of Ahmose (EA3), followed by the remarkable tombs of Beni Hasan.
Overnight: Minya

Day 7 – Sail to Sohag via Asyut

Full day sailing south through Upper Egypt toward Sohag via Asyut.

Distance: Approx. 225 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 31–33 hours (against the current)
Enjoy buffet meals and afternoon tea onboard while cruising.
Overnight: Sohag

Day 8 – Visit Abydos / Sail to Qena

Morning excursion to the magnificent Abydos Temple before continuing south toward Qena.

Distance: Approx. 160 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 22–23 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Qena

Day 9 – Visit Dendera Temple / Sail to Luxor

Visit the beautifully preserved Dendera Temple Complex before sailing onward to Luxor.

Distance: Approx. 65 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 9–10 hours(against the current)
Sunset tea served on deck upon arrival.
Overnight: Luxor

Day 10 – Luxor Sightseeing

Explore the wonders of ancient Thebes including the Valley of the Kings, Mortuary Temple of Hatshepsut, Karnak Temple Complex, and Luxor Temple.
Overnight: Luxor

Day 11 – Sail to Edfu

Continue sailing southward toward Edfu.

Distance: Approx. 110 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 15–17 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Edfu

Day 12 – Edfu Temple / Sail to Kom Ombo & Aswan

Visit the impressive Temple of Edfu before sailing to Kom Ombo for a visit to Kom Ombo Temple and continuing onward to Aswan.

Distance: Approx. 105 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 14–15 hours(against the current)
Farewell dinner onboard.
Overnight: Aswan

Day 13 – Aswan Sightseeing

Visit the remarkable Aswan High Dam, the beautiful Philae Temple, and the fascinating Nubian Museum.
Overnight: Aswan

Day 14 – Disembarkation & Departure

Disembarkation followed by transfer to Aswan Airport for your onward flight home
