Luxor to Cairo Dahabeya Cruise

12 Nights / 13 Days
Experience the timeless beauty of the Nile aboard a traditional Dahabeya, elegantly towed by a support tugboat for long-distance navigation. Throughout your journey, enjoy daily international buffet breakfast, lunch, and dinner, along with a daily sunset afternoon tea service with snacks served on deck while sailing or at your overnight mooring.

Please note: Sailing times below are estimated for a traditional Dahabeya sailing northbound from Luxor to Cairo with the Nile current, resulting in approximately 15–20% shorter sailing times compared to the southbound Cairo-to-Luxor itinerary.



Day 1 – Arrival in Luxor / Embarkation

Arrival in Luxor and transfer to your Dahabeya for embarkation. Welcome dinner onboard.
Overnight: Luxor



Day 2 – Valley of the Kings & Hatshepsut Temple

Discover the wonders of the West Bank including the Valley of the Kings and the spectacular Mortuary Temple of Hatshepsut.
All meals onboard including sunset tea with snacks.
Overnight: Luxor



Day 3 – Karnak & Luxor Temples / Sail to Qena

Visit the monumental Karnak Temple Complex and Luxor Temple before beginning your northbound Nile journey toward Qena.

Distance: Approx. 65 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 7–8 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Qena



Day 4 – Dendera Temple / Sail toward Sohag

Visit the beautifully preserved Dendera Temple Complex before continuing northbound along the Nile.

Distance: Approx. 160 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 17–19 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Sohag



Day 5 – Visit Abydos / Sail to Asyut

Excursion to the magnificent Abydos Temple followed by continued sailing toward Asyut.

Distance: Approx. 100 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 11–12 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Asyut



Day 6 – Sail to Minya

Full day leisurely sailing north through Middle Egypt toward Minya while enjoying panoramic Nile scenery from the sundeck.

Distance: Approx. 125 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 14–15 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Minya



Day 7 – Tel El Amarna & Beni Hassan

Explore Tell el-Amarna including the Northern Tombs and the Tomb of Ahmose (EA3), followed by the impressive tombs of Beni Hasan.
Overnight: Minya



Day 8 – Tuna El Gebel & Ashmunein / Sail to Beni Suef

Visit Tuna El Gebel and Ashmunein before continuing northbound toward Beni Suef.

Distance: Approx. 145 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 16–17 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Beni Suef



Day 9 – Fayoum Excursion

Excursion to the ancient pyramids of Hawara Pyramid and Meidum Pyramid.
Sunset tea and dinner onboard.
Overnight: Beni Suef



Day 10 – Sail from Beni Suef to Cairo

Continue your scenic Nile voyage northward toward Cairo through the fertile landscapes of Middle Egypt.

Distance: Approx. 120 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 13–15 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Cairo



Day 11 – Cairo Sightseeing

Visit the legendary Giza Pyramids and the renowned Egyptian Museum.
All meals onboard including sunset tea with snacks.
Overnight: Cairo



Day 12 – Old Cairo & Disembarkation Preparations

Optional exploration of Old Cairo, Khan El Khalili Bazaar, or leisure time onboard.
Farewell dinner onboard.
Overnight: Cairo



Day 13 – Departure

Transfer to the airport for your international departure flight
