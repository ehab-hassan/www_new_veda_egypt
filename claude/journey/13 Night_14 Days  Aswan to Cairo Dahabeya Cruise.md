Aswan to Cairo Dahabeya Cruise

13 Nights / 14 Days

Discover the entire length of the Nile aboard a traditional Dahabeya featuring daily international buffet breakfast, lunch, and dinner, as well as a daily sunset afternoon tea with snacks served during sailing or at scenic moorings along the riverbanks.



Day 1 – Arrival in Cairo

Arrival and transfer to your hotel.
Overnight: Cairo

Day 2 – Fly to Aswan / Embarkation

Flight to Aswan and embarkation aboard your Dahabeya.
Overnight: Aswan

Day 3 – Aswan Sightseeing

Visit the High Dam, Philae Temple, and the Nubian Museum.
Overnight: Aswan

Day 4 – Sail to Kom Ombo & Edfu

Sail northward to Kom Ombo with a visit to Kom Ombo Temple before continuing onward to Edfu.

Distance: Approx. 105 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 12–13 hours(with partial current assistance)
Overnight: Edfu

Day 5 – Edfu Temple / Sail to Luxor

Visit Temple of Edfu before sailing to Luxor.

Distance: Approx. 110 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 13–14 hours
Overnight: Luxor

Day 6 – Luxor Sightseeing

Visit the Valley of the Kings, Temple of Hatshepsut, Karnak Temple, and Luxor Temple.
Overnight: Luxor

Day 7 – Sail to Qena / Abydos & Dendera

Continue northward to Qena with excursions to Abydos and Dendera Temples.

Distance: Approx. 65 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 7½–8 hours (with the current)
Overnight: Qena

Day 8 – Sail to Sohag via Naga Hammadi Bridge

Distance: Approx. 160 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 18–19 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Sohag

Day 9 – Sail to Minya

Full day sailing north along the Nile Valley.

Distance: Approx. 225 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 26–28 hours (with the current)
Overnight: Minya

Day 10 – Tell El Amarna, Tuna El Gebel & Ashmunein

Explore Tell El Amarna and its Northern Tombs including the Tomb of Ahmose, followed by Tuna El Gebel and Ashmunein.
Overnight: Minya

Day 11 – Beni Hassan / Sail to Beni Suef

Visit Beni Hassan before continuing north.

Distance: Approx. 145 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 16–18 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Beni Suef

Day 12 – Fayoum Excursion / Sail to Cairo

Visit Hawara and Meidum Pyramids before sailing onward to Cairo.

Distance: Approx. 120 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 14–15 hours(with the current)
Overnight: Cairo

Day 13 – Cairo Sightseeing

Tour the Giza Pyramids and the Egyptian Museum.
Overnight: Cairo

Day 14 – Disembarkation & Departure

Transfer to the airport for your international departure flight
