Cairo to Luxor Dahabeya Cruise

12 Nights / 13 Days

Experience the timeless beauty of the Nile aboard a traditional Dahabeya, elegantly towed by a support tugboat for long-distance navigation. Throughout your journey, enjoy daily international buffet breakfast, lunch, and dinner, along with a daily sunset afternoon tea service with snacks served on deck while sailing or at your overnight mooring.

Please note: Sailing times below are calculated for a traditional Dahabeya under tow and therefore are considerably longer than conventional Nile cruise ships.



Day 1 – Arrival in Cairo / Embarkation

Arrival in Cairo and transfer to your Dahabeya for embarkation. Welcome dinner onboard.
Overnight: Cairo

Day 2 – Cairo Sightseeing

Visit the legendary Giza Pyramids and the renowned Egyptian Museum.
All meals onboard including sunset tea with snacks.
Overnight: Cairo

Day 3 – Sail from Cairo to Beni Suef

Begin your Nile journey sailing south toward Beni Suef through the fertile landscapes of Middle Egypt.

Distance: Approx. 120 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 16–18 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Beni Suef

Day 4 – Fayoum Excursion

Excursion to the ancient pyramids of Hawara Pyramid and Meidum Pyramid.
Sunset tea and dinner onboard.
Overnight: Beni Suef

Day 5 – Sail to Minya / Tuna El Gebel & Ashmunein

Continue sailing south to Minya followed by visits to Tuna El Gebel and Ashmunein.

Distance: Approx. 145 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 19–21 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Minya

Day 6 – Tel El Amarna & Beni Hassan

Explore Tell el-Amarna including the Northern Tombs and the Tomb of Ahmose (EA3), followed by the impressive tombs of Beni Hasan.
Overnight: Minya

Day 7 – Sail to Asyut

Full day sailing south toward Asyut.

Distance: Approx. 125 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 17–18 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Asyut

Day 8 – Sail to Sohag / Visit Abydos

Continue southbound to Sohag with an excursion to the magnificent Abydos Temple.

Distance: Approx. 100 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 14–15 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Sohag

Day 9 – Sail to Qena

Leisurely sailing day toward Qena while enjoying panoramic Nile scenery from the sundeck.

Distance: Approx. 160 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 21–23 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Qena

Day 10 – Dendera Temple / Sail to Luxor

Visit the beautifully preserved Dendera Temple Complex before continuing toward Luxor.

Distance: Approx. 65 km
Estimated Sailing Time: Approx. 9–10 hours(against the current)
Overnight: Luxor

Day 11 – Valley of the Kings & Hatshepsut Temple

Discover the wonders of Valley of the Kings and the spectacular Mortuary Temple of Hatshepsut.
Overnight: Luxor

Day 12 – Karnak & Luxor Temples / Disembarkation

Visit the monumental Karnak Temple Complex and Luxor Temple prior to disembarkation.
Farewell dinner onboard.
Overnight: Luxor

Day 13 – Departure

Transfer to the airport for your international departure flight.
