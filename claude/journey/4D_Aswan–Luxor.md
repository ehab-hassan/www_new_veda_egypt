Aswan–Luxor Luxury Sailing Experience
3 Nights / 4 Days



Day 1 | Aswan → Kom Ombo

12:00 – Embarkation aboard your elegant dahabiya, where timeless charm meets the gentle rhythm of the Nile. Settle into your cabin and embrace the calm, refined atmosphere
12:00–13:00 – Lunch on board, featuring a curated selection of fresh local and international cuisine
13:00–17:00 – Guided visit to the enchanting Philae Temple, set on Agilkia Island and dedicated to Isis, offering a serene and picturesque introduction to Upper Egypt
17:00 – Sunset Afternoon Tea on Deck, a relaxing daily ritual with tea, coffee, and light delicacies as the Nile glows in golden hues
18:00 – Begin your sailing journey towards Kom Ombo. Enjoy the silence of traditional navigation—no engine noise, only the soothing sounds of water and breeze
19:00–20:00 – Dinner on board in an intimate setting
Overnight – Moored near Kom Ombo beneath a starlit sky


Day 2 – | Kom Ombo → Edfu → Luxor

06:00–07:00 – Breakfast on board with tranquil sunrise views
07:00–08:00 – Visit the unique Kom Ombo Temple, dedicated to Sobek and Horus, known for its symmetrical design and riverside setting
08:00 – Sail towards Edfu, enjoying a peaceful cruise past palm groves, fertile fields, and traditional villages
12:00–13:00 – Lunch on board
13:00–15:00 – Explore the remarkably preserved Temple of Edfu, one of the most complete temples in Egypt, rich in carvings and history
15:00 – Continue sailing towards Luxor via Esna, with uninterrupted moments of relaxation on deck
17:00 – Sunset Afternoon Tea on Deck, a perfect pause to enjoy the scenery and soft evening light
18:00–20:00 – Dinner on board
Overnight – Arrival and overnight stay in Luxor


Day 3 – Luxor – West & East Banks

05:00–05:30 – Early breakfast
05:30–12:00 – Explore Luxor’s West Bank, including the Valley of the Kings, where ancient pharaohs were laid to rest in beautifully decorated tombs carved into the hills
12:00–13:00 – Lunch on board
13:00–17:00 – Visit Karnak Temple Complex, a vast and awe-inspiring site of monumental architecture, followed by Luxor Temple, gracefully located along the Nile
17:00 – Sunset Afternoon Tea on Deck, offering a serene moment to unwind after a day of exploration
19:00–20:00 – Dinner on board
Overnight – Stay in Luxor


Day 4 – Tuesday, 10 November| Departure

06:00–07:00 – Breakfast on board
08:00 – Check-out and disembarkation, concluding your tranquil sailing experience
